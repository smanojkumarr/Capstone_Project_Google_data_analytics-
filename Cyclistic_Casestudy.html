Summary of the Project - Case Study¶

The Cyclistic is a fictional bike share company in Chicago. The director of marketing believes the company’s future success depends on maximizing the number of annual memberships. for this we need to design a new strategy to increase the number of annual memberships. We need to analyze and visualize the analysis.

Stakeholder¶

Lily Moreno: The director of marketing and your manager.
Cyclistic Executive Team: Decision-makers for approving marketing strategies
Cyclistic Marketing Analytics Team

About the company¶

Company Started Year: In 2016
No.of Bicycles : 5,824 bicycles
No.of Stations : 692 stations
Mode of Usage : Unlocked from one station and returned to any other station in the system anytime
Pricing Plans : Single-ride passes - Full-day passes - Annual memberships
Cyclistic members : Customers who purchase annual memberships
Aimed : Converting casual riders into Annual members

  1. How do annual members and casual riders use Cyclistic bikes differently?

Usage Pattern per Month¶

In [19]:
df['start_month'] = df['start_time'].dt.month

casual_data = df[df['usertype'] == 'Customer']
member_data = df[df['usertype'] == 'Subscriber']


casual_monthly_counts = casual_data['start_month'].value_counts().sort_index()
member_monthly_counts = member_data['start_month'].value_counts().sort_index()


plt.figure(figsize=(13, 6))
plt.plot(casual_monthly_counts.index, casual_monthly_counts.values, marker='o', label='Customer')
plt.plot(member_monthly_counts.index, member_monthly_counts.values, marker='o', label='Subscriber')
plt.title('Usage Pattern per Month')
plt.xlabel('Month')
plt.ylabel('Number of Trips')
plt.xticks(casual_monthly_counts.index, labels=['Jan', 'Feb', 'Mar', 'Apr', 'May', 'Jun', 'Jul', 'Aug', 'Sep', 'Oct', 'Nov', 'Dec'])
plt.legend()
plt.grid(True)
plt.show()

Usage Pattern Throughout the Year¶

Lower demand during the winter months - Weakest Months (January, February, November, December)¶

  • Possible influence of adverse weather conditions, such as intense cold.

Demand Increase from March: There was a recovery in usage from March onwards.¶

Peak Usage : June and September : The summer months see the highest levels of usage.¶

Recommended actions:¶

  • Can implement the maintenance activity during the lower usage months (January, February, November, December).
  • Provide offers and discounts during the lower usage month to encourage the usage.
